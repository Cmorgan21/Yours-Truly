Eyelash Extensions

Lash Extensions are single strands of synthetic eyelashes that are curved to replicate a natural eyelash. They are applied to each individual natural eyelash one by one. There are four types of eyelash extensions.
These consist of Classics, Hybrids, Russian and Mega-Volume

  • Classics - The most natural form of Lash Extensions and the least expensive. One Lash Strand is attached to each individual lash
  • Hybrids - Considered generally between natural and a bit more 'fluffy'. They can be passed off as a natural look and perfect for going out
  • Russain Volume - Russian Volume are considered the mots popular form of lash extensions. Being the most flexible when it comes to different styles of extensions and a more full appearance
  • Mega Volume - The fluffiest of all! Mega - Volume is not short of Bold. It is the biggest and boldest extension there is.

Eyebrow Shaping

There are a variety of methods for Eyebrow Shaping. Eyebrow shaping is generally a quick and easy process. Here are some of the methods

  • Waxing - Waxing is the quickest and most effective method for shaping. It can cause a slight discomfort during treatment, bbut takes 2-3 weeks for the hair folicle to grow back
  • Threading - Threading is another method to eyebrow shaping. Threading is a very quick process. The hairs are pulled out by a thread. Howvever the only downside to threading is your hairs may come back wirey.
  • Plucking - Plucking is a very common method people use. Pulling a hair out one by one to shape their eyebrows. You can be very precise with plucking, however it can be very time consu3ming - with hairs growing back quickly.

Hereis an order a facial consists of:

Filler

Dermal filler is a form of hylaronic acid injected under the skin. Dermal filler creates a smoother or fuller appearance to areas desired. Filler has a hard like feel for the first 2 weeks Post Treatment then begins to soften. Some of the areas that can be treated are:

  • Lips - Lip Filler are one of the most known treatments for Filler. Giving the lips more definition or more fullness. With a range of different techniques
  • Jaw - Jaw Filler enhances the sharpness of the jaw. Making it look more defined and giving a slimmer appearance
  • Nose - Nose filler typically used for smoothing out someones side profile, but also front profile.
  • Nasobiala Folds - Typically for lightening the lines and wrinkles around the smile line area
