Eyelash Extensions

Lash Extensions are single strands of synthetic eyelashes that are curved to replicate a natural eyelash. They are applied to each individual natural eyelash one by one. There are four types of eyelash extensions.
These consist of Classics, Hybrids, Russian and Mega-Volume

  • Classics - The most natural form of Lash Extensions and the least expensive. One Lash Strand is attached to each individual lash
  • Hybrids - Considered generally between natural and a bit more 'fluffy'. They can be passed off as a natural look and perfect for going out
  • Russain Volume - Russian Volume are considered the mots popular form of lash extensions. Being the most flexible when it comes to different styles of extensions and a more full appearance
  • Mega Volume - The fluffiest of all! Mega - Volume is not short of Bold. It is the biggest and boldest extension there is.
